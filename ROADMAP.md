# KEY Foundation Roadmap

## Guiding Principle

> KEY does not seek to reduce storytelling to formulas. It seeks to identify the persistent structures that allow creativity to produce coherent, meaningful, and surprising narratives. Every claim remains provisional and is expected to evolve through observation, comparison, and evidence.
